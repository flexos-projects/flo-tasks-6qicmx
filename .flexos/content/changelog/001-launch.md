# Flo is live

After three months of building, testing with 40 beta users, and more invoice-chasing jokes than we can count — Flo is open to everyone.

## What's in v1

- **Clients** — full contact management with billing details
- **Projects** — fixed price and hourly, with budget tracking
- **Tasks** — kanban board within each project
- **Time tracking** — running timer + manual entry, billable flag
- **Invoices** — generated from time entries, PDF, send by email
- **Dashboard** — live view of everything outstanding

## What's coming next

- Client portal (view and pay invoices without an account)
- Recurring invoices for retainer clients
- Stripe payment links
- Xero / QuickBooks export

Thank you to everyone who helped shape it. Go send some invoices.
