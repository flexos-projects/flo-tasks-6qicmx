# Home Page

The only marketing page that matters for SEO. Should convert a freelancer who finds it via "freelancer invoice tracker" search.

## Sections

1. **Hero** — "Get paid. Not chased." — headline that names the pain. CTA: "Start free"
2. **Problem** — three pain points: scattered tools, late invoices, time wasted on admin
3. **Product walkthrough** — 3-step: add client → log time → send invoice. Screenshots.
4. **Social proof** — 3 testimonials from real users (content/testimonials collection)
5. **Pricing CTA** — simple "from £12/month, no contracts" link to /pricing
6. **Footer** — links, legal

## SEO targets

- "freelancer invoice software"
- "time tracking for freelancers"
- "freelancer project management"

## Copy direction

Dry, direct. No "streamline your workflow". Real language: "Stop chasing invoices", "Know exactly what you're owed".
