# Vision

## The Problem

Freelancers are great at their craft. They're terrible at admin — not because they're disorganised, but because the tools don't fit how they actually work.

Invoicing tools assume you're an accountant. Project management tools assume you have a team. CRM tools assume you're in sales. Freelancers get squeezed between all three and end up with a mess of spreadsheets, Notion pages, and late invoice reminders.

The result: hours lost to admin, invoices sent weeks late, clients left in the dark, and money left on the table.

## The Solution

Flo is a single workspace for the complete freelancer workflow. A client comes in, a project is created, tasks are tracked, hours are logged, and an invoice is generated — all in one place, all connected.

No imports. No exports. No switching tools.

## Who it's for

**Primary user:** Independent freelancers billing 3–10 clients at any time. Designers, developers, writers, consultants. Solo or with 1–2 collaborators. £3k–£15k/month revenue. Currently using a combination of Notion + FreshBooks + Google Sheets.

**Secondary user:** Small agencies (2–5 people) who want lightweight project tracking without enterprise overhead.

## Core principles

1. **Follow the money** — every workflow eventually ends at an invoice. Flo makes that path frictionless.
2. **Client-first structure** — everything is organised around clients, not projects or tasks. That's how freelancers think.
3. **Zero admin overhead** — if Flo creates admin, it's failing. Time logging should take 10 seconds. Invoicing should take 60.

## Success in 2 years

Flo is the default tool for freelancers who take their business seriously. 50k active users, average 4.2 clients per user, NPS > 65. Known for the best invoice-to-payment flow in the market.
