# Content Overview

## Collections

### changelog
Product update announcements. Published on the marketing site and shown in-app on the "What's new" banner.

**Fields:** title, description, body (markdown), version, publishedAt, tags
**Used on:** /changelog page, in-app update banner
**Cadence:** Roughly bi-weekly

### testimonials
Customer quotes for the marketing landing page.

**Fields:** quote, authorName, authorTitle, authorCompany, avatarUrl, featured
**Used on:** Home page social proof section
**Source:** Collected manually via email after user hits 3-month mark

## Content strategy

Marketing copy (hero text, feature descriptions, pricing) lives directly in the page specs, not in collections — it changes infrequently and is owned by the same person writing the specs.

Collections are for dynamic content that changes independently of the product design.
