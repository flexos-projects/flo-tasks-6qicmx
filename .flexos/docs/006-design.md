# Design Overview

## Design philosophy

Flo is a daily-use tool. The design should feel calm and efficient, not impressive. Every pixel earns its place by helping a freelancer move faster.

Dark mode by default — freelancers work late. Dense but not cluttered — they have a lot of data to scan. Zero decorative elements — no gradients in the app, no illustrations, no marketing language inside the product.

## Brand personality

**Professional without being corporate.** Confident, direct, a bit dry. The brand says "I've got this" not "We're excited to help you on your journey."

## Visual system

- **Primary:** Violet (#8B5CF6) — distinctive, not the usual SaaS blue
- **Surfaces:** Very dark, near-black — app feels like a proper tool
- **Typography:** Inter — readable at small sizes, neutral, modern
- **Data density:** Medium-high — freelancers are scanning, not browsing

## Accessibility

WCAG 2.1 AA. Time entries and invoices involve numbers — contrast is non-negotiable.
